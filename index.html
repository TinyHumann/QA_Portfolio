Why Hire Me

I am a quality-focused QA Engineer with strong experience across manual, automation, and mobile testing, capable of operating effectively in fast-paced, remote-first product teams. I approach testing from both a technical and business-risk perspective, ensuring critical user journeys, integrations, and edge cases are validated before release.

My background in fintech and e-commerce testing has equipped me to work with complex workflows such as onboarding, authentication, payments, and data validation across multiple devices and environments. I collaborate closely with developers and product managers, providing clear, actionable defect reports that accelerate fixes rather than slow teams down.

I take ownership of quality outcomes, continuously improve test coverage through automation where it adds value, and advocate for scalable QA practices that support long-term product growth.

What I Bring in My First 30 Days

Week 1: Product & Quality Understanding
Quickly ramp up on the product, user personas, and business objectives. Review existing documentation, requirements, and test assets while setting up local and CI testing environments. Identify high-risk areas and clarify quality expectations with the team.

Week 2: Test Coverage & Process Alignment
Design or refine test plans and test cases aligned with real user flows. Begin executing exploratory and regression testing, ensuring defects are logged with clear reproduction steps, impact analysis, and priority recommendations.

Week 3–4: Automation & Quality Optimization
Contribute to or extend existing automation frameworks using industry-standard tools. Focus automation efforts on stable, high-value scenarios while improving overall test efficiency. Proactively suggest process improvements to reduce release risk and increase delivery confidence.

By the end of the first month, I aim to be a fully contributing QA partner — adding measurable value through improved coverage, reduced defects, and stronger collaboration across the engineering team.
